# Espacio Interior — Digital Advertising & Growth Strategy

**Context:** Grace P. Pacheco, Psicóloga Clínica (USFQ), bilingual ES/EN therapy practice.
Target markets: Ecuador (primary), Latin America (secondary). Spanish-first, English-capable.

---

## 1. Google Ads vs. Facebook Ads — Platform Comparison

| Factor | Google Ads | Facebook / Meta Ads |
|---|---|---|
| User intent | **High** — searching for help NOW | **Low/medium** — passive scrolling |
| Avg. CPC (mental health, global) | ~$3.74 | ~$1.58 |
| Avg. CPC (Ecuador / LatAm est.) | ~$0.50–$1.00 | ~$0.20–$0.50 |
| Best for | Appointment bookings, direct calls | Brand awareness, trust-building, retargeting |
| Ad format | Text search ads | Image, video, carousel, Stories |
| Audience targeting | Keyword intent | Demographics, interests, behaviors |
| Therapy fit | Captures people in crisis / actively seeking | Nurtures undecided, reaches stigma-hesitant |
| Privacy sensitivity | Medium | Higher (Meta tracks broadly) |

**Key insight:** Ecuador CPC is ~82% below US rates on both platforms. A budget that gets 20 clicks in the US gets ~110+ clicks in Ecuador.

**Recommended budget split: 60% Google / 40% Meta** — shift to 50/50 once retargeting audiences build up.

## 3. Full Channel Strategy

### A. Google Ads (Search) — Demand Capture
- Campaign type: Search
- Geo: Ecuador (primary); Colombia, México, Perú (secondary if budget allows)
- Budget: start at $150–$200/mo; scale from conversion data
- Goal: WhatsApp click or "Agendar sesión" button = conversion event
- Ad copy — lead with the problem, not the service:
  - **Headline:** *¿Sientes ansiedad o estrés que no puedes controlar?*
  - **Description:** *Psicóloga clínica bilingüe. Sesiones online, confidenciales. Agenda hoy.*
- Extensions: Call (WhatsApp number), Sitelinks (Servicios, Sobre mí, Preguntas frecuentes)

### B. Meta / Facebook + Instagram Ads — Awareness & Retargeting
- Campaign types:
  1. **Awareness** — video or image, top of funnel, soft CTA
  2. **Retargeting** — pixel-based, target website visitors who didn't book
  3. **Lead gen** — Facebook Lead Form (lower friction than website form)
- Placements: Instagram Stories + Reels (highest LatAm engagement), Facebook Feed
- Targeting: Women 25–45 in Ecuador; interests: bienestar, salud mental, meditación, libros de autoayuda; life events: new relationship, divorce, job change
- Creative: Short-form video (30–60s Reels) of Grace speaking to camera — authenticity outperforms polished production for therapy
- Budget: $75–$100/mo to start

### C. Website (espacio-interior.ec)
- Add Meta Pixel + Google Analytics 4 with conversion events (WA click, form submit)
- Add Google Tag Manager for clean event management
- Key conversion points: WhatsApp floating button, "Agenda una sesión" CTA, contact form
- Blog/SEO: 1–2 posts/month targeting long-tail keywords → organic traffic compounds
- Schema markup: `MedicalBusiness` / `LocalBusiness` with Ecuador address for local SEO

### D. WhatsApp Business
- Primary conversion destination — LatAm users strongly prefer WhatsApp over email or forms
- WhatsApp Business quick-reply buttons: "Quiero saber más", "Agendar sesión", "Ver precios"
- Auto-response for after-hours messages
- All paid ads should link to WhatsApp, not just the website form
- Track WhatsApp clicks as Google Ads / Meta conversion events

### E. Substack
- Long-form trust-building channel — not a sales channel
- Content: mental health education, reflections, "preguntas frecuentes reales de clientes"
- Cadence: 2x/month
- Promote via: Instagram bio link, Facebook page, email signature
- Cross-post excerpts to Instagram as carousels (high save/share rate)
- Add subscribe CTA to website footer and blog posts

### F. Instagram / Facebook Organic (Meta)
- Content mix: 70% educational, 20% personal/behind-the-scenes, 10% promotional
- Formats: Reels (reach), Carousels (saves/shares), Stories (daily engagement)
- Hashtags: `#saludmental` `#terapiaonline` `#psicologaonline` `#bienestaremental` `#ansiedad` `#quito` `#ecuador`
- Cadence: 3–4x/week Instagram, 2x/week Facebook

### G. Google Business Profile (free, high ROI)
- Create/claim: "Espacio Interior — Psicóloga Online"
- Category: Psychologist / Mental Health Service
- Add photos, hours, WhatsApp link, service descriptions
- Collect Google Reviews — shows in Maps + "near me" results

## 4. Funnel Architecture

```
Cold Audience
│
├── Instagram Reels / Meta Awareness Ads
│       └── Organic content (trust-building)
│
└── Google Search Ads (high intent)
        │
        ▼
    Website (espacio-interior.ec)
        │
        ├── Blog post / SEO page
        ├── Services page
        └── Contact / CTA
                │
                ├── WhatsApp conversation → Booking
                ├── Contact form → Email → Booking
                └── Substack subscribe → Nurture → Booking
                        │
                     Retargeting
                     (Meta Pixel)
```
